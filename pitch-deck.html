06
WHY AN MP CARES

Every question becomes early-warning intelligence

Each advisory drops an anonymized pin on the constituency map. When pins cluster, the office gets an outbreak alert — and can send a KVK team or relief funds to the exact block before a local problem becomes a crisis.

  • 📍
    Real-time, ground-truth crop health by village
  • 🎯
    Target MPLADS spending where evidence shows need
  • 🤝
    Augments extension officers — never replaces them
⚠️ Outbreak alert: 7 reports of Soybean — Yellow Mosaic Virus around Ausa block in the last 3 hours. Recommend dispatching KVK Latur team.

10
WHY THIS, NOT THE OBVIOUS BUILD

Not a chatbot. Not a leaf classifier.

The obvious version

Upload a photo → get a disease name in English text on a screen. Assumes literacy, a smartphone, and stops at the individual.

Kisan Alert

Photo + spoken question → spoken, actionable fix in the farmer's language → aggregated into live MP intelligence. Reaches the excluded, and helps a whole constituency.

Grounded in field data, honest about uncertainty, and safe with chemicals — the details judges probe, answered by design.
